Social Journalist

About


Social Journalist' are the essential volunteers of "freedom of speech". They are the absolute defenders of democratic rights of the people they live in, live with, live for.

Social Journalist' are based within the people they want to serve. Usual media houses are constrained with self censorship in reporting News. Social Journalist' have the option to overcome bias, self-censorship, thereby report News with required honesty. Social Journalist do journalism out of conscious. Their News are authentic.

Newslets is a Platform for free minded people who want to be socially responsible in their actions. We urge you to do the job for your own satisfactions of serving your people where you live with people, you love the location. You want development of the people and the location.

You can do social journalism in two ways :

1. Remain Anonymous by using a Username which do not take your Name and Location.

Example >
sjid@Like2Report sjid@LetTruthComeOut sjid@journalist4life
sjid@printer3490 sjid@GoodWors99 sjid@reporter3201

2. Be Known by using your Name and Location (where you will be reporting from).

Example >
sjid@MikeOhara/London sjid@chandaJ/Lusaka
sjid@HR/Dhaka/BD sjid@HunSenMing/Rangoon

Who will be your Followers ?
Local people will Follow you for News as if you are their only news channel. People who are away from the location for different reasons will Follow you for News. Organizations and businesses of the Location will seek news from you. People from overseas who originate from this location will follow you. All people who came into contact of this location will Follow you for News. People always look for News from the places they have most reference level and empathy. Moreover, foreign media houses (online and offline) always seek local news they cannot cover for cost reason. You will be astonished to see the width and breath of readership of your News.

Newslets will send your News to all of your Following and all of your Followers. So we guarantee 100% readership on each News you publish. Newslets has another unique method in developing your Follower baseline. Headline of your News will be send to Universal Stream as Microblog. If anyone click on the Headline Platform will render whole News. Many will send Follow request from headlines thus enlarging your Followers news by news.

What are your benefits ?
Over a period you will become a seasoned journalist who will be a specialist on your location. You will become you Own News Channel. You stand a chance of becoming Newslets Graduated Journalist who will be beneficiary of revenues from local ads. In case your news becomes World News of the day, you will receive a payment from Newslets. Moreover Newslets will have several formats of news distribution for specific readership on daily basis. Inclusion of your news will be paid for.
