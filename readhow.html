Newslets


100%+ Organic Exposure of your Posts



Newslets was designed as the "equal opportunity distributor of communication".This premise is realized by our Posts Flow Model.

Newslets will distribute Posts from any category of User to 100% of Following and 100% of Followers. No User will have to pay in any form, either Ad or Promoted for the Posts to flow unrestricted. Nor Newslets will ever impose any conditions on free flow of Posts or Communication. This is purely in honor of "Freedom of Speech" that we believed in totality.

Moreover, Newslets will send Headlines of each Post as Microblog made by any user to Platform's Universal Stream (to all Users who are not in your Following and Followers baseline). This situation creates an opportunity for the Headline to be Clicked by unlimited number of Users. Once clicked on the Headline, Platform will render full Post for reading. Good Headline with relevant memorable texts and visual will fetch many Follower requests. Any Follow request that originates is accepted by Default. So the potential of growing Followers by Headlines are enormous. This is how the process becomes 100%+.
