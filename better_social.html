Then you can expand your size of horizon as you prepare to put more time in to relations to get more benefits from more and more Following and Followers.

    2. Content or Post Reader Page contains a Follow button by the side of Content Creator's Identification. Following are the ways you can reach to those Pages :

      > Content and posts Feed Column (2nd column of your dashboard). Click to save the content/post on READER at 3rd column. Click on EXPAND to view the Content/Post. Make a Follow move if you find the originator of the content is of quality (judge from the quality of content). Follow only when you think this user may serve your purpose of following.

      You have the options to change Feeds of 2nd Column. Selectors Tabs are at the 1st Column which once selected by Category, Feeds changes according to your wishes.

    3. Headlines of your Posts becomes Microblog and flows to Universal Stream. Your Posts have tremendous potential to grow Followers from that natural organic scope embedded in the Platform.

    It is evident from the above examples of Post Flow, that, quality of contents/messages in each post is the key to growth of Followers.
